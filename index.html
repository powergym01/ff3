What is Fat Freezing

Fat freezing, or cryolipolysis, is a non-invasive procedure that targets and eliminates stubborn fat cells by applying controlled cooling. The body naturally removes the frozen fat cells over time, resulting in a slimmer appearance without surgery or downtime.

How Fat Freezing Works

Fat Freezing targets and cools fat cells to a temperature that triggers their natural death. Over the following weeks, your body processes and eliminates these dead fat cells, revealing a more toned and sculpted figure

Your Fat Freezing Journey

Initial Consultation: Meet with Dr. Jaison and his team to discuss your goals and assess the areas you want to target.

Personalized Treatment: During the session, a specialized applicator is applied to the targeted area, delivering controlled cooling to freeze the fat cells.

Natural Elimination: Over the next few weeks, your body gradually processes and eliminates the treated fat cells, resulting in noticeable fat reduction
